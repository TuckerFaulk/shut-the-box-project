How to play?


Objective

The object of the game is to shut, or lower, as many number tiles as possible during your turn.


Game Play

The game begins with all the number tiles in the up position. The player will roll two dice and add them up. The player will then lower an equal amount of tiles.


For example, if the player rolls a total of 9, they can lower any tiles equal to 9:

  • 9
  • 8 and 1
  • 7 and 2
  • 6 and 3
  • 5 and 4
  • 6, 2, and 1
  • 4, 3, and 2

The player continues to roll as long as they have an equal number of tiles to lower. When no more tiles can be lowered, the turn is over and the game is reset.
