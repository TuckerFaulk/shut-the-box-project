How to play?


Objective

The object of the game is to get numbers on the dice that exactly add up to the value that is required to click the number blocks, and finally shut the box.


Game Play

The game begins with all the number blocks visible and then the dice are rolled. The player can then click number blocks which total to the value of both dice.


For example, if the player rolls a total of 9, they can click any number blocks equal to 9:


  • 9
  • 8 and 1
  • 7 and 2
  • 6 and 3
  • 5 and 4
  • 6, 2, and 1
  • 4, 3, and 2

The player continues to roll as long as they have an equal number of blocks to click. If you roll a number without the required number blocks available, you go bust.
But if you manage to click all number blocks, you win the game!!!
